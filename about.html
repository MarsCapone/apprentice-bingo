Apprentice Bingo

AFAIK, Apprentice Bingo was a game created by Muzer, piersmw, and thomasleese to play during seasons of The Apprentice. The data used to generate the bingo cards can be found in the original repo.

This site is a simple web app which allows you to generate bingo cards in browser, and use them while watching (no printer required). It is my first foray into AngularJS.
